YOU ARE ENOUGH. YOU ARE VALUABLE. YOUR STORY IS MEANT TO BE HEARD. YOU WERE CREATED TO LIVE A LIFE WHICH IS FULLY ALIVE, IN EVERY ASPECT.

As we gather together with the women of JC Sacramento, our heart is to remind one another that no matter what your story is, it is powerful.

By sharing and listening to each other’s stories, having real conversations, and connecting over the course of 5 weeks, our hope is that we would overcome fear, rejection, comparison and disappointment. Together we will instill a sense of sisterhood, growth, and grace for the process.

We are gathering to remind one another that we are not alone, we are not stuck, and there is hope and freedom for everyone, in every situation.

We were never meant to just get by, barely make it or even just survive. As sons of God, we were created to live fully alive in every area of our lives. He has called us to live abundant lives.

As we gather together the men of Jesus Culture Sacramento for 5 weeks we will dive into what it looks like to live fully alive and talk about some of the potential things that could prevent that. Living a life fully alive is full of adventure and vulnerability. Our hope is to create space for open and honest conversations as we challenge and encourage one another to grow in our identities in the Lord, as sons.
