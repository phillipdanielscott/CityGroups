YOU ARE ENOUGH. YOU ARE VALUABLE. YOUR STORY IS MEANT TO BE HEARD.

As we gather together with the women of JC Sacramento, our heart is to remind one another that no matter what your story is, it is powerful.

By sharing and listening to each other’s stories, having real conversations, and connecting over the course of 5 weeks, our hope is that we would overcome fear, rejection, comparison and disappointment. Together we will instill a sense of sisterhood, growth, and grace for the process.

We are gathering to remind one another that we are not alone, we are not stuck, and there is hope and freedom for everyone, in every situation.
